How to take care of your dog:

Taking care of a dog involves providing proper nutrition, fresh water, regular exercise, and routine veterinary check-ups to keep them healthy. Grooming, including brushing and bathing, helps maintain their coat and skin. Training and socialization are essential for good behavior and mental stimulation. Ensuring a safe environment, regular playtime, and affection strengthen the bond between owner and pet. Lastly, keeping up with vaccinations, parasite control, and regular health monitoring ensures a long, happy life for the dog.
